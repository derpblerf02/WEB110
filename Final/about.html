The Prompt Method I Built

Slot > Stuff

Use distinct headings—Foreground, Midground, Background, Style. These create clear “token islands” the AI can target without cross‑talk.

Constraint First

Front‑load objective details—camera, lens, lighting—before subjective mood. This stops stylistic overrides from bleeding into core composition.

Iterate in Minutes

Need just a lighting tweak? Swap that single slot and regenerate. The structured layout keeps every other aesthetic choice locked.
