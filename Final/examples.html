From Vague Ideas to Precise Results

The difference you see in the examples isn't luck; it's structure. Giving the AI a clear blueprint eliminates guesswork and unlocks its true potential. Here’s why it works:

Instead of one confusing sentence, you direct the AI layer by layer. By defining the Foreground, Midground, and Background, you get precise control over the scene's depth and focus.

Vague prompts like "a broken building" lead to generic images. The structured format pushes you to provide the rich, actionable details—like "detailed crumbling concrete, exposed rebar, shattered windows"—that produce cinematic, high-quality results.

Separating Style from content is crucial. It allows you to define the mood—like "gritty dystopian"—without it interfering with the scene's details. You control the vibe and the visuals independently.

Want to change the lighting or the background? With a structured prompt, you just edit that specific section. This makes refining your idea simple and predictable, saving you time and credits.
