Why This Format Works

Hierarchical Structure: By organizing your prompt into Foreground, Midground, and Background, you mirror how visual composition actually works. Midjourney's AI can better understand the spatial relationships and importance of each element.

Detailed Context: Instead of just saying "fuzzy duck," we provide specific details within each layer. "Foreground: adorable duckling [soft yellow down feathers, bright orange bill, sitting in shallow pond water, morning dewdrops on feathers]" gives the AI clear, actionable information.

Style Separation: By isolating style directives from content, you avoid conflicts between what you want and how you want it rendered. This prevents the AI from getting confused about whether "dark" refers to the subject matter or the lighting style.

Consistency & Iteration: The structured format makes it easy to modify specific aspects without rewriting the entire prompt. Want to change just the background? Simply edit that section while keeping everything else intact.

AI Training Alignment: This format aligns with how modern AI models process visual information - in layers and with clear hierarchical understanding. It speaks the AI's "language" more effectively than stream-of-consciousness descriptions.

❌ Lazy Prompt Problems

  • Ambiguous visual hierarchy
  • Mixed style and content directives
  • Lacks specific visual details
  • Inconsistent results
  • Hard to iterate and refine

✅ Structured Prompt Benefits

  • Clear visual composition
  • Separated concerns
  • Rich, specific details
  • Predictable, high-quality results
  • Easy to modify and improve
